Kuan-Ju Wu, born in 1985, received his Master in Tangible Interaction Design from the Carnegie Mellon University, Pittsburgh, PA in 2009 and his Bachelor in Electronic Engineering from National Chiao Tung University, Taiwan in 2007. He has shown at Frame Gallery, Pittsburgh, Assemble Gallery, Pittsburgh, Interacción I/O/I, Disseny Hub Barcelona, and others. He is now serving as Technology Lead for the experience design company Tellart LLC.

Kuan-Ju’s work explores the aesthetics that result from constructive processes and tangible interaction between people and kinetic systems.
