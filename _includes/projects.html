Hi! My name is Kuan-Ju Wu and I'm a creative technologist and new media artist.
I design devices and narratives at the interaction of art, technology and ecology.
For the past 10 years, I have been active in experiencial design industry (Tellart), HCI research and teaching (UC Berkeley), and new media art. My experties are prototyping with emerging technologies (both HW and SW), material exploration, and concept development.
Currently I'm interested in 3 things:
1. Developing a library of morphing mechanism with soft materials for my creative practices.
2. Creating speculative stories about the symbiotic relationship between human, machine and nature.
3. Building playful interactive experiences that sparks curiousity, raises ecological sensitivity, or cultivate joy in our daily life.
Please feel free to reach me for a chat or collabration!
